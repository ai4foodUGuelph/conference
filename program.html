Event Program

October 1 & 2, 2025

The event will bring together a curated group of thought leaders and practitioners from government agencies, academia, food production companies, AI and startup communities, and technology solution providers, along with international experts. The participants will encompass a diverse range of expertise, including food and agriculture, policy, engineering, computer science, social sciences, ethics, and more.

** Please note this program is subject to change.
