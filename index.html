"Welcome to the AI National Dialogue"

National Dialogue on
AI in Food Safety

The University of Guelph (AI4Food and Research Innovation Office), along with the Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA), are organizing the second event in the National Dialogues on AI in Agriculture and Food Series entitled the National Dialogue on AI in Food Safety. This event will be held on June 4 and 5, 2025 at the University of Guelph, Guelph, Canada. It aims to foster cross-sector conversations exploring the transformative potential of AI in enhancing food safety while addressing ethical, technical, and regulatory challenges.

Event Themes

  • Food identity

    Fraud detection, traceability, and supply chain transparency.

  • Food quality/contamination

    AI-driven quality control measures, real-time monitoring, and data analytics.

  • Food waste

    Using AI to optimize supply chains, reduce spoilage, and minimize food waste.

  • Food supply chain resilience

    Enhancing system resilience through predictive analytics and risk modeling.
